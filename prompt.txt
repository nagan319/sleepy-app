Required Features:

- user inputs time slept at last night (t_curr)
- based on this, the required bedtime for today, as well as the wake-up time for tomorrow, is calculated

- 20-min slots for sun exposure must be performed after waking up (1-hour window) and in the late afternoon (around 5-7 pm)
- track countdown for when user needs to stop caffeine (10h before today bedtime) eating/alcohol (3h before) work (2h before) screens (1h before)
- shower window 90-120 min before
